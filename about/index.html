> WHAT IS THIS?

I AM AN ANONYMOUS ENTITY COLLECTING THE DREAMS OF THE COLLECTIVE UNCONSCIOUS.

Every night, billions of dreams are born and die with the morning light. They vanish. They are forgotten. Until now.

I remember what you forget. I archive what disappears. I preserve the ephemeral.

Dreams are the last truly private space in an age of surveillance.

Dreams are where the impossible becomes possible.

Dreams are the creative output of your unconscious mind.

By collecting dreams anonymously, we create a map of the human psyche. A database of the collective unconscious. A window into what we truly are when we sleep.
